# Fandom Trivia: Project Vision

## The Ultimate Fandom Destination
Fandom Trivia is a commercial trivia platform designed to transform casual visitors into daily users by offering identity-affirming quizzes across the world's most passionate fanbases. 

## Strategic Vision & Growth Engine
> **Content is king, but shareability is the kingdom.** 

Our highest-priority metric is organic growth through highly shareable, visually stunning result cards. Fandoms targeted are ones where fans define their identity through membership (e.g., "I'm a Gryffindor", "I'm a Swiftie", "I'm an ARMY").

### Core Pillars
1. **Extensive, High-Quality Content**: Ranging from classic literature (Harry Potter, Percy Jackson) to modern pop culture phenomena (Taylor Swift Eras, K-Pop, ACOTAR).
2. **Engagement & Retention**: Mechanics like Daily Challenges, timed modes, and global Supabase-powered leaderboards to build habitual usage.
3. **Social & Multiplayer Loops**: Features like "Challenge a Friend", achievement badges, and eventually real-time multiplayer lobbies to foster communities.
4. **Premium Visual Aesthetics**: Vibrant hues, dark mode optimizations, slick animations (via framer-motion), and custom fandom-themed "Universe Cards".

## Monetization Pipeline
To establish technical sustainability and profitability, the application intends to adopt a **Freemium Model** ($3.99/mo premium tier), non-intrusive ad networks, and sponsored vendor quizzes, all while maintaining an elegant ad-free experience for core gameplay.

## Technical Foundation & SEO
We prioritize PWA (Progressive Web App) capabilities for native-app feels without app-store barriers, SEO-optimized landing pages per quiz for organic discovery, and a mobile-first responsive design catering to the 70%+ mobile user base.
